Wiki/Biography

Kori Sampson was born in 1995 (age 25 years; as in 2020) in Plymouth, Devon, UK. He did his schooling from Devonport High School for Boys, Plymouth, England and holds a degree in Nutrition.

Physical Appearance

Height (approx.): 5’ 7”

Eye Colour: Dark Brown

Hair Colour: Brown

Family & Ethnicity

Kori’s father’s name is Mark Sampson, and his mother’s name is Nikki Davis. He has a sister named Tilly.

Career

He was a trainer at his local PureGym. The experience that he gathered at PureGym helped him in becoming a personal trainer. He has modelled for brands like Luxe, Line, and Ruff Elegance.

He has also modelled for the designer Samuel Hogue. He made his entry in episode 6 of the show, Too Hot To Handle (2020), and was eliminated in episode 8 of the show.
