365 DNIs Michele Morrone Channels James Bond at No Time to Die Premiere

365 DNI‘s Michele Morrone looks as suave as James Bond while attending the No Time to Die world premiere at Royal Albert Hall on Tuesday evening (September 28) in London, England.

The 30-year-old actor and model was joined by Caroline Scheufele, the artistic director and co-president of Chopard.

“We all have a secret to protect. What’s yours? It was amazing to represent @chopard at the premiere of 007 in London,” Michele posted on his Instagram account.

Michele spent the past few months filming the sequel to 365 DNI, which is currently untitled. It is expected to be released sometime in 2022. Stay tuned as we learn more!

The film features a brand new character and fans wondered if there was something going on between the two stars in real life.

FYI: Michele is wearing a Versace suit and Chopard rings and watch.
