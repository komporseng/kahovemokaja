How much are tickets to watch the 2024 Paris Olympics?

The 2024 Paris Olympics is set to be an exciting event, showcasing the world's best athletes against the iconic backdrop of the River Seine and the Eiffel Tower. Here's a breakdown of the ticket prices and availability for the highly anticipated Games.

Official Ticket Sales and Options

  • Tickets for the 2024 Paris Games are currently available on Paris2024.com, the only official site for ticket sales. It is important to note that purchasing tickets from unauthorized sites is done at one's own risk.

  • The official Hospitality provider for the Paris 2024 Olympic Games, On Location, offers Hospitality and Travel Packages. These packages include premium seating for sporting events, exclusive access to in-venue lounges, museum tours, and hotel stays, among other amenities.

  • An official resale ticket site is set to launch in Spring 2024, providing fans a second chance to purchase tickets for their favorite sporting events.

Ticket Prices and Availability

  • Single ticket prices for the Paris 2024 Olympic Games will range from EUR 24 to 950 (excluding ceremonies). Notably, more than one million tickets will be offered at EUR 24, with nearly 50% of tickets reserved for the general public priced at EUR 50 or less. Additionally, almost a third of the general public tickets for medal events will be offered at EUR 100 or less.

  • The rates are made possible by an adapted pricing policy, which allows for 15% of the general public tickets to be offered at the highest rates, making millions of tickets available at lower prices.

  • There will be a resale site where attendees can purchase tickets when it launches in the spring, providing another opportunity to obtain tickets for the Games.

Specific Event Ticket Prices

Opening Ceremony

  • Ticket prices for the Opening Ceremony range from around $98 to nearly $3,000. As of the publication time, all but the most expensive tickets appear to be sold out.

Gymnastics

  • For the gymnastics qualification round, a Section D ticket sells for around $26, while the same seat in the Finals costs around $109. Front row seats in the A-section start at $109 and reach a peak of $684.

Track and Field, Sailing, and Other Events

  • Attendees can purchase packages for track and field games, hospitality services, and access to exclusive perks. These packages are currently available for Track and Field and Sailing, providing additional details for those interested in premium services.

Urban Sports (Breaking, Skateboarding, 3x3 Basketball)

  • Thousands of new tickets for urban sports at the Paris 2024 Olympics are available at La Concorde. Breaking, Skateboarding, and 3x3 Basketball tickets are priced between €24 and €190.

Multi-Ticket Packages and Club Paris 2024

  • For the first time, the general public has the opportunity to purchase a multi-ticket package of up to three sessions, with prices starting at EUR 72. Fans are encouraged to become members of Club Paris 2024 to receive updated ticket information and have the opportunity to win priority access when sales open.

The 2024 Paris Olympics offers a range of ticket options, from single event tickets to multi-ticket packages and premium hospitality packages. With various price points and availability, fans have the opportunity to experience this historic event in person. It's important to stay updated with official ticket information and make purchases through authorized channels to ensure a memorable and secure experience at the Games.
