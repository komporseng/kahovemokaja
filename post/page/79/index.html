Gwen Adshead, 60, has spent 30 years working in hospitals alongside inmates like Peter Sutcliffe and documents her experience in her book The Devil You Know.  Located in Crowthorne, Berkshire, Broadmoor Hospital has housed dozens of sadistic killers since it opened its doors in 1863, including Yorkshire Ripper Sutcliffe, gangster Ronnie Kray and rapist Robert Napper.
