KUWTK Season 9 Preview: Kris Jenner &amp; Bruce Jenners Shocking Kiss

Kris and Bruce Jenner have a make out session in the new trailer for 'KUWTK' and we want to know if you think they're back together! Bruce Jenner and Kris Jenner look totally in love in the season 10 trailer of Keeping Up With The Kardashians. But this is coming from an estranged couple, or so we thought. It looks like this footage is a bit old since it's captioned "the best" and then later they show "the worst." It definitely has us wondering if there will be a reconcilliation on the new season.

Kris Jenner & Bruce Jenner — Are They Back Together?

Kris and Bruce announced on season nine of KUWTK that they were going to live separately, although they were not divorcing. But in the trailer for the new season which premieres on June 8, we see Kris and Bruce lock lips. 

Could this mean they are back together?

Bruce & Kris Show PDA At Airport

When Bruce and Kris got back from their trip to Thailand on April 2, which was filmed for KUWTK, they were holding hands at LAX. They looked so happy together. Far from the days when they announced their separation in October of 2013.

We should also point out that on their 23rd wedding anniversary, April 21, Kris wished Bruce a happy anniversary on Twitter!

“Happy Anniversary to one of the most amazing men I know!!!!!” Kris wrote. “I love you, Wow 23 years!!!! #love #weareofficiallyvintage.”

This definitely has us wondering if they are back together or perhaps it was all for the storyline of the show? Nothing is ever what it seems!
