Meet Sherri Kramer,  Bob Saget Ex-Wife

Sherri Kramer earned prominence as the first wife of late comedian Bob Saget.

Bob discussed his first marriage in his memoir “Dirty Daddy”. He wrote that he and his ex-wife met each other in high school when he was 17 years old. The pair started dating and got married in 1982. In September 1990, Bob and Sherri had purchased a two-story Mediterranean property is $2,995,000.

However, after 15 years of marriage and three children, Bob and Sherri called it quits.

They got their divorce in 1997 where their divorce documents cited “irreconcilable differences” as the cause of their split. According to Sun-Sentinel, Sherri was the one who filed for the divorce. The filing came a few months after Bob had announced that he wouldn’t return as the host of “America’s Funniest Videos”.

After the divorce, Bob had said that revealed to Closer Weekly, that he never thought he would have a relationship again.

Sherri Kramer And Bob Saget Kids

Sherri shared three kids with Bob; twins Aubrey Saget and Lara Melanie Saget born on 16 October 1989 and Jennifer Belle Saget on 18 November 1992.

US Weekly reported that Bob had sent a text to his daughter Aubrey Saget before his death on 9 January 2022. Aubrey shared a screenshot of the message on her Instagram story “Thank u. Love U. Showtime!”

Moreover, talking about Sheeri’s other daughters, Lara works as an artist, curator, and educator. She attended New York University and earned her masters in 2018. Also, she bagged two bachelor’s degrees from Barnard College and Columbia University. She currently resides in New York, according to her Facebook.

Sherri and Bob’s daughter Jennifer currently lives in San Luis Obispo, California. Her IG Bio reads, she is a “Lover of music, art, technology, science, potatoes, monkfish liver, octopus, firefly squid”.

As for Aubrey, she earned her BFA and MFA from New York University. As a painter, she had a solo exhibition in 2015, 2017, and 2019. She had also done tons of group exhibitions and selected curatorial projects. She had won New York University Steinhardt Masters in Fine Art Scholarship Award Grant in 2015.

Moreover, Aubrey got engaged to her boyfriend Andy Kabel on 20 December 2020. He announced on his Instagram, “I said yes“. She has also introduced her boyfriend to her mother Sherri.

Sherri and her daughters were recently spotted together in 2019 attending an event.

What Does Sherri Kramer Do For A Living?

Besides her law career, Sherri had also tried her hand as a screenwriter. She received credit for helping write, “Bob Saget: In the Dream State”.
