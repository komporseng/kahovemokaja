BIG KIDS: KIERSEY CLEMONS STARS IN NEW VID

Actress/recording artist Kiersey Clemons is starring as the love interest in a new Trey Songz music video for his song “Smartphones.”

The song is from Trey Songz new album called “TRIGGA.”

Kiersey is best known for her recurring role on “Austin & Ally as Kira Starr and Danielle on Disney channel series “Shake It Up.” She was recently the star of the hit Disney channel original movie “Cloud 9” an inspirational film set in the competitive world of snowboarding, which premiered in Latin America on May 11th, 2014.

She is also an independent recording artist and is working on an album that is set to be released this summer.
