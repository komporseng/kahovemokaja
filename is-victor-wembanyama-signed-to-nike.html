Is Victor Wembanyama signed to Nike?

Victor Wembanyama, the 7'4" basketball prospect, has signed a multi-year sneaker deal with Nike as he prepares to enter the NBA as the potential #1 pick in the 2023 Draft. Wembanyama's journey with Nike began during his professional career in France, where he wore custom Nike shoes throughout the season. This endorsement deal with Nike is set to make history, potentially becoming the largest sneaker deal for any rookie in history, with a reported value of $100 million. This deal signifies a significant milestone in Wembanyama's career, solidifying his status as a top athlete and aligning him with one of the most iconic sports brands in the world.

Wembanyama's initial foray into the world of endorsements and sponsorships occurred as he embarked on his professional career in France, where he first signed a multi-year footwear and apparel deal with Nike. This early partnership laid the foundation for his current status as a Nike athlete, poised to make a splash in the NBA. As he transitions to the league, Wembanyama is expected to continue showcasing Nike footwear, leveraging his association with the brand to further elevate his presence in the basketball world.
