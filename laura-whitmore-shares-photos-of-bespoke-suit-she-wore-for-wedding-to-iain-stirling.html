Laura Whitmore shares photos of bespoke suit she wore for wedding to Iain Stirling

She tied the knot with beau Iain Stirling last year and recently welcomed a baby girl.

And Laura Whitmore, 35, has now shared details about the ‘beautiful journey’ and design process behind the chic look she created for her Dublin wedding. 

Taking to Instagram on Friday, the Irish presenter revealed her white suit was made by Joshua Kane bespoke tailoring and she opted for Jimmy Choo shoes which had her new initials LWS on them.

Work in progress: Laura Whitmore has shared details about the design process behind the chic look she created for her Dublin wedding last November

In a series of snaps shared to her social media page, Laura was seen posing for mirror selfies while at fittings for the sophisticated suit.

Another picture showed the lace for her shirt being expertly placed before Laura tried on the statement top under her stunning fitted blazer.

Showing her involvement at every stage of the ensemble’s creation, Laura also posted a drawing of desired outfit complete with a cutting of lace and two buttons. 

Laura also revealed that her husband Iain ensured to pull out all the stops for their wedding day by donning special socks with their faces on. 

Stunning: The Irish presenter revealed her white suit was made by Joshua Kane bespoke tailoring as she shared several pictures showing the intricate lace and impressive design

‘I wanted something I was comfortable in, could dance around in but also felt incredible! It was perfect.

Laura first spoke about her and Iain’s wedding, gushing that they had the ‘most magical, perfect ceremony’, to celebrate the start of 2021.

It was revealed in November that the presenter and comedian had secretly married in Dublin, a month before their baby news was announced.

Taking to Instagram over New Year’s, Laura confirmed the nuptials and said they ‘loved having it to ourselves’ – while sharing a beautiful snap from the couple’s big day.

Sharing some details about their wedding, Laura penned: ‘Entering 2021 like… 

Amazing: The mother-of-one said her outfit was ‘perfect’ as she shared more details about the look including that her bag was her ‘something borrowed’

Dream team:  The chic look was created by Joshua Kane bespoke tailoring

Dream design: Showing her involvement at every stage of the ensemble’s creation, Laura also posted a drawing of desired outfit complete with a cutting of lace and two buttons

‘A year ago Iain asked me to marry him and we had the most magical, perfect ceremony. We loved having it to ourselves. 

‘We had been planning the perfect celebration and I will forever be so thankful that’s what we got. We have never spoken publicly about our engagement or wedding. 

‘Back at the time we never felt the need to say anything as it’s all such a personal experience. We also were too busy enjoying it! 

‘We’ve never shared this picture before now but a lot of people have contacted us and congratulated us the last month with good intentions so here’s to good news!  

Laura finished up her post by wishing her fans a happy New Year and ‘sending love to you and yours’. 

Special keepsake: Laura revealed she had received a stunning illustration of her wedding look from Gillian Bell on Friday morning, sparking the trip down memory lane

It had previously been reported that Laura and Iain tied the knot in November at a small ceremony at Dublin’s City Hall.  

They reportedly wed in front of 25 guests – the maximum allowed under Ireland’s level five rules at the time – with Laura’s half-brother Adam and Iain’s sister Kirsten as witnesses. 

Shortly after their marriage was revealed, Laura shared the news of her pregnancy on Instagram. 

Laura and Iain welcomed their first child, a daughter, in March but have yet to reveal publicly the little girl’s name.

Laura posted the first image of her newborn baby girl to Instagram last month, with the image showing her cradling her little one in a sling for the sweet snap. She wrote alongside: ‘thanks for all the kind messages at this time. We are in love x.’  

While it is not known exactly when she gave birth, Stacey Dooley stepped in to host the presenter’s BBC Radio 5 show last month.   

‘We are in love’: Laura posted the first image of her newborn baby girl to Instagram last month, with the image showing her cradling her little one in a sling for the sweet snap

The TV star revealed she was having a daughter in her self-help book, No One Can Change Your Life Except for You.   

She penned: ‘As I finish this book, it doesn’t end at chapter nine, there’s a new chapter coming into my life – in the form of a baby girl currently growing inside me and that scares the s**t out of me. 

‘I once swallowed a bug and freaked out there was a live bug inside me. A live human is a whole new level of freaking out but I am excited because there’s a lot of good in the world, there’s a lot of potential.’

Reflecting on impending motherhood, Laura said: ‘I want to bring up my child knowing that she has her own story to write. She will be supported and loved. But she is the heroine of her own life, no-one else.’  

Lorraine Kelly had originally let slip that Laura was set to welcome a daughter, noting that the mother-to-be will likely want to pass advice on to her ‘baby girl’.
