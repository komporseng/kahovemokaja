Marie Avgeropoulos Age, Biography

Marie Avgeropoulos was born on June 17, 1986, and is currently 36 years old. She was born in Thunder Bay, Ontario, Canada, where she grew up and spent her childhood. She is of Canadian nationality and follows the Christian faith. She spent a few years in Europe when she was 18 years old. She presently divides her time between Los Angeles and Vancouver.

Marie received her first schooling at a nearby elementary school in Ontario. She then attended a local middle school, and after finishing middle school, she enrolled at the Port Arthur Collegiate Institute (currently known as Lakehead University Law School). She began taking studies in Broadcast Journalism after graduating from high school and went on to obtain her main degree in that field. She then attended Confederation College in Thunder Bay, Ontario.

Career

Marie’s career took off when she was cast in the 2014 sci-fi post-apocalyptic television series The 100. She rose to prominence for her depiction of Octavia Blake.

She went on to appear in films and television shows such as I Love You, Beth Cooper (2009), Percy Jackson & the Olympians: The Lightning Thief (2010), Wonder Woman: Bloodlines (2019), Dead Rising: Endgame (2016), Harper’s Island (2009), Supernatural (2009), Human Target (2010), Sorority Wars (2009), and others.

She was nominated for a Leo Award for Best Supporting Performance by a Female in a Motion Picture for her outstanding performance in Numb. She will be seen in the upcoming films Butterfly in the Typewriter, Super Dicks, and King of Killers, an action thriller.
