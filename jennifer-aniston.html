Career

Jennifer Aniston started her career as a theatre artist and worked in popular plays like For Dear Life and Dancing on Checker’s Grave. She also worked at several part-time jobs to support herself. She worked as a telemarketer, waitress, and bike messenger.

After that in the year 1988, she received a minor role in the science-fiction series titled Mac and Me. After that, she worked as a spokesmodel for popular fitness brand Nutrisystem on The Howard Stern Show.

Since then she has received some notable roles in various TV series like Molloy, Ferris Bueller, Camp Cucamonga, The Edge, Muddling Through, Quantum Leap, Burke’s Law, and many more TV serials. Raising to popularity Jennifer also made her big-screen debut with the popular horror film Leprechaun in the year 1993.

Unluckily, her initial television career doesn’t go well but in the year 1994, her television career got a huge breakthrough as she received an offer to work in the popular television series Friends which telecasted for continuous 10 years. She rose to immense popularity for her role as Rachel Green in this show and has won many prestigious awards for her incredible acting in this popular show.

Not only in the United States, her role in the series Friends gained her worldwide recognition and she also established herself as one of the recognized actresses in the Hollywood industry. After that, she marks her return on the big screen with her appearance in the movie titled She’s the One and then worked in movies like Picture Perfect, The Object of My Affection, Office Space, and many more popular films.

In the year 2013, she gave her biggest commercial success movie titled Bruce Almighty. After the year 2005, she has given back to back successful films like Rumor Has It, The Break-Up, Room 10, Marley Me, He’s Just Not That Into You, The Bounty Hunter, Just Go With It, and many other successful movies.

In the year 2019, Jennifer marks her return on the television screen with her appearance in the Apple TV+ series The Morning Show.

She has also been featured in the musical comedy Dumplin in the year 2018 on Netflix.

Along with her filming and television career, she has also been featured in some popular videos for Microsoft’s Windows 95 release, L’Oreal hair products advertisements, SmartWater campaign, and others. She also co-founded some well-known brands like Lolavie perfume, J perfume, Living Proof hair care, and also become the brand ambassador of Emirates airline in the year 2015.

She also co-founded a production company named Plan B Entertainment in the year 2002 along with her ex-husband Brad Pitt. Later in the year 2008, she founded a company named Echo Films along with producer Kristin Hahn. Along with her professional career, she also works as a philanthropist and worked with many charitable organizations. She has completed many philanthropic works for different causes and also donated a huge amount for several other causes.
