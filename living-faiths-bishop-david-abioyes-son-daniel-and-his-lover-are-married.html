Living Faiths Bishop David Abioyes son, Daniel and his lover are married

Daniel Abioye, son to Bishop David Abioye of Living Faith Church, aka Winners’ chapel and his lover, Temilola Adeleye are married.

According to Charming Magazine, “The classy and God ordained event took place at Classique Event center, number 7A Kudirat Abiola way, Oregun Ikeja.”

Temilola Adeleye is the daughter to one DCN, Olajide and Nkese Adeleye, who are based in the United Kingdom.

Odunlade Adekola biography, wife, children, age, movies,  net worth

Talk of one of the most popular and celebrated actors in Nollywood, and Odunlade Adekola will make it to the top 10.

The actor who started as a Yoruba actor has, over the years, also taken part in English movies.

He is known for his comic characters. And his fans continue to rate him as the Nigerian king of meme.

Odunlade has, over the years, been able to build a name for himself. He goes by different names, which include; Sunday Dagboru, Mufu Olosha Oko, Samu Alajo, among other.

Apart from acting, Odunlade is a good singer and has a list of tracks to his name.
