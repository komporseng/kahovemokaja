MAFS bride Domenica Calarco gives an update on police investigation against Olivia Frazer

Married at First Sight star Domenica Calarco has confirmed the police investigation into her nude photo leak is on hold after Olivia Frazer refused to speak to police.

The Sydney-based influencer, 29, revealed on Tuesday the case against her former co-star had ‘stalled’ because Olivia and other parties exercised their legal right not to talk to police, meaning there is no new evidence to go on.

‘The police are just still investigating it. [The matter] has gone on for a really long time,’ she explained.
